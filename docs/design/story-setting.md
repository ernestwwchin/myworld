# MyWorld RPG — Story & Setting

## Tone

Dark fantasy dungeon crawl. No overworld — just **town → dungeon → town**.

## World Structure

- One portal world per campaign (e.g. "Goblin Invasion")
- Each world has **10 depth floors** with a boss floor at depth 5 and a shrine/ending at depth 10
- **Town hub**: Safe zone with portal plaza, quest keeper NPC, and quartermaster. Player starts and returns here between runs.

## Dungeon Floors

| Floors | Theme |
|---|---|
| 1–4 | Goblin/skeleton pool, dark lighting |
| 5 | Hand-crafted boss stage (Goblin Captain + guards) |
| 6–9 | Undead/demon pool, darker theme |
| 10 | Hand-crafted shrine — special dialog, reward, ending |

Floors are a mix of hand-crafted and procedurally generated, depth-banded by difficulty and enemy theme.
