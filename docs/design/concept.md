# MyWorld RPG — Concept

A single-player dungeon crawler with **BG3-style tactical combat**, **D&D 5e rules**, and **procedurally generated floors**.

The player runs from a town hub into a dungeon world, descends through depth-banded floors, completes side quests, and survives to extraction. Death and victory both return you to town with loot resolution.

## Inspirations

- **Baldur's Gate 3** — UI layout, combat flow, engage opener, flee mechanic
- **D&D 5e SRD** — Ability scores, dice, attack/AC, skills, status conditions
- **Classic roguelikes** — Fog of war, permadeath pressure, floor-by-floor progression
